About Vornyx Global

Connecting Africa to the World

A dual-service company combining Tours & Travel with Overseas Employment Facilitation — built on transparency, trust, and results.

Our Mission

To Open Doors for
Kenyan Workers

Vornyx Global was founded to solve a real problem: too many Kenyans are being misled, overcharged, or scammed by unscrupulous recruitment agents. We exist to be the opposite — honest, structured, and genuinely helpful.

We focus on placing workers in verified positions in the UAE, Saudi Arabia, Qatar, Oman, and Kuwait, while building towards expansion into Australia, Germany, and the UK.
